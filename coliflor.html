Coliflor

Ingredients


  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Cauliflower
  • 500 grams of panela cheese
  • 500 grams of tomatoes
  • 2 Serrano peppers
  • 500 milliliters of oil
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of anise

RECIPE


  1. Wash the cauliflower and disinfect it. Then, we put a saucepan on the fire with enough water and, when it boils, add the cauliflower. We also add a teaspoon of anise, a clove of garlic, a piece of onion and a tablespoon of salt. Let it boil for five minutes, turn off the heat and proceed to remove the cauliflower sprigs so that they drain well.
  2. In another saucepan, we cook the tomato with water, a clove of garlic, and a piece of onion to be able to prepare the broth that will accompany the weathered cauliflower.
  3. In the blender we are going to grind the tomato, garlic and onion, along with the water where they were cooked. Then, we put a saucepan over low heat, add a drizzle of oil and fry the tomato puree. In addition, we add the serrano peppers, to which we will have made a cross-shaped cut at the tip previously.
  4. We split the cheese into sticks and place it between the sprigs of the cauliflower.
  5. We then proceed to separate the whites from the yolks. Then, we beat the egg whites until they rise to the point of nougat and, later, we will add the yolks one by one, to prevent the egg whites from going down. Once the yolks are well integrated, we begin to place the cauliflower to weather or coat it.
  6. We put the pancakes on a tray so that the excess oil drains. To the tomato broth that we have in the fire, we add salt and a few sprigs of parsley.
  7. Now add the cauliflower pancakes to the previous broth and let it boil for about 10 minutes. We serve the cauliflower with the very hot tomato sauce and enjoy this exquisite dish.
