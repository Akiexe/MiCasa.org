Mi Abuelita

My grandma, Rebeca, was born in Tampico, Mexico. She is kind hearted and her love language has always been to make food. Food was a form of not just love but introduction for her culture as it is not only delicious but holds a lot of history of her own heritage. When she taught me how to cook, it opened my eyes that even complicated recipes can be done in an easier way in order to get the same delectable result. Not just that, but a happy heart as well.
